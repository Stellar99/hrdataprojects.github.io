HR ATTRITION ANALYSIS USING POWER BI

In my role as an HR Analyst, I was enlisted to address the ongoing challenge faced by a company characterized by a gradual attrition and depletion of its workforce over time. Following a meticulous analysis, our recommendations centered on devising comprehensive initiatives aimed at bolstering employee engagement and satisfaction. These initiatives include but are not limited to organizing regular feedback sessions to solicit employee input, instituting robust Compensation and Benefits packages to incentivize loyalty, allocating resources towards Training and Development programs to nurture skill enhancement and career growth, fostering a clear path for advancement opportunities within the organization, conducting thorough exit interviews to glean insights into employee departures, and implementing a system for recognizing and rewarding employees for their exceptional contributions and achievements. By embracing and implementing these multifaceted strategies, the company can anticipate a tangible reduction in attrition rates while simultaneously cultivating a more resilient, committed, and engaged workforce. This proactive approach not only mitigates the risk of talent loss but also lays the foundation for a culture of continuous improvement and sustained success within the organization.
